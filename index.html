RUSTICA KITCHEN / ROSEMARY & THYME

Rosemary & Thyme is a beloved boulangerie and cafe in Newport, RI serving a selection of gourmet coffee, freshly baked pastries, stuffed croissants, and artisan bagels.

Rustica Kitchen takes R&T's former lunch menu and expands it with inspiration from around the globe, with both a takeout menu and full seated service.

Chef/Owners Matt & Anna Tscheulin welcome you to visit Rosemary & Thyme, a  small artisan style bakery and cafe located in historic downtown Newport, RI, as well as our newest venture — Rustica Kitchen. All our baked goods and pastries are scratch made daily in our kitchen. All daily leftovers are donated to our local food pantry. Come spend some time with us!
